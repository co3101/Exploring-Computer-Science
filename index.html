FRIENDS

Friends is a TV show that was created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman on September 22, 1994. The Sit-Com takes place in NYC following the lives of six young friends who are trying to navigate work, love, and paying the bills. The original cast includes Jennifer Anniston (playing the character Rachel Greene), Courtney Cox (playing the role of Monica Geller), Lisa Kudrow (playing the role of Pheobe Buffay), Matt LeBlanc (playing the role of Joey Tribbiani), David Schwimmer (playing the role of Ross Geller), and Matthew Perry (playing the role of Chandler Bing). The show takes the audience on a wild ride of how each one of the friends grow as individuals and together.
